### Information We Collect

"Personal data" as defined by PDPA includes any information directly or indirectly related to an individual and identifiable from that information, including sensitive personal data and opinions about you.

Axrail may collect personal data in various ways, including through online forms for ordering products or services and other instances where users voluntarily provide this information. Types of personal data we may collect include:

- **Identification information:** name, date of birth, identification documents (e.g., NRIC or passport number), gender, nationality, language preference, and contact details (e.g., address, phone number, email).
- **Transaction information:** transaction history, credit/debit card details, bank account information, payment history, and service status.
- **Device and location information:** location or device information, IP addresses, browser type, ISP, and usage data through cookies and log files.
